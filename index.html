Who we are

Founded in 1808 in Germany and acquired by the Lantal Group in 2017, Gierlings Velpor has more than 200 years combined experience in its history. Gierlings Velpor operates one of the few vertical mills left in Europe that emcompasses weaving, dyeing, printing and finishing, including a laboratory.

Our core values have remained the same over the years - We want to establish long-term relationships with our clients and suppliers, putting their success in the centre of all activities. We look forward to the future and to keep bringing aesthetics and functionality in harmony.
